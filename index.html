For your internal discussion

Questions your team will ask. Answered.

Won't this make the BI team redundant?
No — and this is worth saying clearly. Milo handles the repetitive commercial requests that currently eat analyst time: benchmarking, operator comparisons, ad-hoc game performance pulls. The BI team gets that capacity back for the work that actually needs them — data modelling, architecture, and analysis that requires real expertise. The best BI teams we work with use Milo to become more strategic, not less relevant.
We already have Power BI. Why isn't that enough?
Power BI shows what happened. It can't tell you why, and it can't answer a question it wasn't pre-built to answer. Every new question becomes a report request back to the BI team. Milo handles the follow-up — the questions that come up live in a meeting when someone says 'but why did that happen?' It doesn't replace your Power BI dashboards. It handles everything they can't.
What data leaves our environment?
Query results are processed in our Frankfurt AWS environment — EU data residency, ISO 27001 certified, no OpenAI or external model training. Full infosec documentation available on request. Your data stays in the EU and is never used to train models.
How long does integration actually take?
For aggregation tables via MariaDB: typically 2–3 days from credentials to first queries. The 2-week POC starts on aggregations so you get value immediately. Raw spin data via streaming or change-detection runs in parallel and is scoped during the pilot.
